Vision-Mission

vision

Our vision is to create a dynamic and inclusive campus environment where every student is empowered to explore their passions, broaden their perspectives, and develop into socially responsible leaders. Through a rich tapestry of extra and co-curricular activities, we aim to foster a community that celebrates diversity, creativity, and academic excellence. By nurturing a culture of lifelong learning and personal growth, we aspire to equip our students with the skills and values needed to thrive in a global society.

Mission

The mission of the Extra Curricular & Co Curricular Activities Committee is to enhance the educational experience of our students by providing a robust platform for intellectual, cultural, and personal development. We achieve this by organizing a wide range of activities including workshops, seminars, lectures, cultural festivals, and community engagement initiatives. These activities are designed to complement formal academic learning, encourage critical thinking, and promote holistic well-being. By instilling a sense of social responsibility and leadership, we prepare our students to make meaningful contributions to their communities and the world at large.

Extra Curricular & Co Curricular Activities Committee

The committee aims to provide a holistic approach to professional higher education by creating an intellectually and aesthetically stimulating atmosphere on campus. Regular extra and co-curricular activities, along with workshops, seminars, lectures, and demonstrations, are organized to sensitize the academic community to diverse dimensions of human experience and knowledge. These activities enrich students' lives, fostering awareness of social responsibilities and the implications of their actions. The committee's primary responsibilities include ensuring the smooth conduct of ANNUAL DAY celebrations and selecting student teams for Inter Collegiate Cultural festivals.

Objectives:

  • To recognize and appreciate the dormant multi-talents of students.
  • To provide a platform to exhibit and enrich students' skills.
  • To encourage students' multi talents through proper guidance.
  • To develop traits like coordination and team spirit among students.
  • To act as a conduit for translating students' gifted talents into joyful and unique experiences.
  • To initiate student participation through the formation of a student activities club.

Student Integrated Committee

Objectives

  • Induction of 'service' in education for imparting 'education for total transformation'.
  • Development of integrated manpower possessing technological and managerial knowledge and skills; values and ethics needed to make an honorable living and contribute to the socio-economic development and welfare of the society.
  • Student motivation and career guidance.
  • Providing basic infrastructure to old-age homes, orphanages, asylums, and blind schools.
  • Propagating the ideals and benefits of 'right to education' to students of remote areas.
  • Integration of various efforts into a collective whole for the enhancement of efficiency and effectiveness of technical education and welfare activities.
  • Organizing workshops and seminars.
